The course, /MGMT 301: Management Theory & Practice/ is a course at Stage II (that's typically, or ideally, in a student's final year at the [[https://www.auckland.ac.nz/][University]] (if they are doing a major or similar in [[https://web-beta.archive.org/web/20100505010923/https://www.business.auckland.ac.nz:80/][management]].

The course is based around the use of [[https://www.smartsims.com][Mike's Bikes (advanced)]] and seeks to help students reflect on their practice as managers as they work in teams running a simulated bicycle company, and as they compete with other student run companies.
