The main responsibilities of a [[{{< ref "202109111201-project-managers" >}}][project manager]] are [[{{< ref "202109111153-project-management-tasks" >}}][planning and organizing, managing tasks, budgeting and controlling costs, and other factors]]. These tasks require interpersonal skills in order to meet the following responsibilities of a project manager:

- Teaching and mentoring of team members
- Building relationships
- Controlling change in the project
- Empowering the team
- Communicating status and concerns
